Our Programs

Bringing together programs in art, design, photography, publishing, theatre, film, journalism, and web design, Creative Arts & Industries offers practical, hands-on training which prepares students for exciting and fulfilling careers.

Our instructors are artists, designers, performers, writers, and professionals passionate about helping students discover and harness their creativity and providing the industry connections to help them succeed in their field.
