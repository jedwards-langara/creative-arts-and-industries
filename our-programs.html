Our Programs

Get practical, hands-on training with Langara Creative Arts & Industries.

Combine your passion with the technical skills, business knowledge, and experience that you need for a rewarding creative career.

Creative Arts & Industries Programs

Design Formation

Diploma, two-years

  • Two-year foundation diploma in multidisciplinary design
  • Aims to promote a comprehensive understanding of the design process
  • Training across visual merchandising, interior spaces, exhibit and communication design for an entry-level job or further studies in design
  • Students graduate with a diploma and a professional exit portfolio and go on to work in careers in retail visual merchandising, graphic design, interior services, exhibit design, or seek further education in marketing & advertising, illustration, architecture, and digital, graphic, industrial or interior design streams

Learn more about the Design Formation program | Follow Design Formation on Facebook

Film Arts

Certificate, eight months

  • Intensive, collaborative and comprehensive training program for studetns wishing to work in film as actors, writers or directors
  • Choose from three streams: acting, writing, or directing
  • Students are guaranteed to write, direct, or act (depending on stream) in a minimum of two festival quality films
  • Graduates from all streams are making their way in the film industry in numerous ways

Learn more about the Film Arts program | Follow Film Arts on Facebook

Fine Arts

University-transferable Diploma, two years

  • Art + design + intensive + studio + hands-on + community
  • An intense sutdy of visual arts and design that provides students with a wide set of studio skills, as well as the opportunity to specialize in one or more of the studio disciplines
  • Program emphasizes studio skills, with students gaining strong foundation skills in any or all of the following disciplines: drawing, design, painting, sculpture, printmaking, ceramics, digital design, and public art
  • University-transferable courses with many alumni choosing to go straight into their year general fine arts at Kwantlen Polytechnic University and Emily Carr University

Learn more about the Fine Arts program | Follow Fine Arts on Facebook

Publishing

Diploma, one-year (three terms)

  • Uses the latest industry tools and techniques to teach students how to design, write, edit, and create print and web publications
  • Each student graduates with a professional and diverse portfolio that they can proudly present to employers including a new issue of Pacific Rim Magazine and LangaraPRM.com
  • Graduates have successful careers in a diverse range of industries, from art directors in the fashion industry to editors in content marketing and advertising agencies to web designers in leading edge design firms

Learn more about the Publishing program | Follow Publishing on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram
